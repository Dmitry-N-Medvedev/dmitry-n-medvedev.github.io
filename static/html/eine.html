The company
It was a stealth fintech project of my friend Maximillian Power, his father and me.
We wanted to go to not so prosperous countries and help their citizens to comfortably pay their elictricity bills, and health insurance. We would play a role of a proxy that would pay these expenses in advance to elictricity and health insurance providers annually and charge our customers monthly payments.
The margin in price between a health insurance certificate payed annually and monthly would allow us to prosper.
