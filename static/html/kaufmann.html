The context
Restaurants order stuff from their local suppliers - bottled water, carrots, potato, meat, household chemicals - everything.
The ordering process involves giving a call to the supplier and go through a list of items, their amount and specification.
E.g.
A restaurant wants to order water of a specific manufacturer bottled in glass. No plastic. Just because the bottles will be served on tables and it is not nice to serve plastic bottles to guests.
The problems
The communication medium is not reliable enough
This makes it inevitable to constantly double check that each item on the list is understood by the recepient correctly
Either party or both speak very basic German
Not all restaurants are run by native German speakers. Thus a lot of mistakes especially on paronyms are being constantly made and have to be corrected by repeating such items again and again
Changing an order after it has been agreed upon
Oftentimes a restaurant manager has to add items to the order they already have made. Now they need to give a call to their supplier, wait online until the supplier is ready to answer and go with the supplier through the order again to make necessary modifications to it.
